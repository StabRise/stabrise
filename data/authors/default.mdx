At StabRise, we’re transforming how businesses process documents. We specialize in managing both structured and unstructured data, with a strong focus on security and compliance — whether it’s HIPAA, GDPR, or custom privacy regulations, we make sure your documents meet the highest standards.

We pride ourselves on the flexibility and scalability of our solutions. Whether you're processing large DICOM files or massive PDFs, our system handles it all seamlessly. Powered by our open-source projects and robust cloud platforms like Spark, AWS, and Azure, our technology evolves with your business.

In addition to our proprietary solutions, we’re committed to the open-source community. Our own open-source projects provide tools and resources for businesses and developers to tackle complex document processing tasks. From data extraction to anonymization, we give you the power to innovate while keeping control of your processes.

Security is at the core of everything we do. We use the latest encryption and data protection technologies to ensure your sensitive information is secure every step of the way.

At StabRise, we blend cutting-edge technology with a human approach. Our team is here to provide scalable, reliable solutions, with a personal touch, to help you tackle your document processing needs confidently.
